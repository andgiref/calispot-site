Karuanas — Delete Your Account

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

You can delete your Karuanas account and all associated data directly from within the app. No email or support request is needed.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Karuanas app and log in.
  2. Go to your Profile tab.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (email address, display name)
  • All spots you have submitted
  • All photos you have uploaded
  • All reviews you have written
  • Your saved favorites
  • Your authentication credentials

Timing

Account deletion is processed immediately. Once confirmed, this action cannot be undone.

Contact

If you have questions about account deletion or your data, you can reach out at support@karuanas.eu. As a personal project, responses may take a few days.
