What is Karuanas?

Karuanas helps you find outdoor calisthenics and street workout spots wherever you are. Browse a community-driven map of parks, pull-up bars, rings, and workout stations — or add your own in seconds.

Found a great spot that's not on the map? Drop a pin, select the equipment, snap a photo, and share it with the community.

Features

  • Interactive map with clustering and search
  • Filter spots by equipment: pull-up bars, parallel bars, rings, dip stations, and more
  • Community photos and reviews
  • Save your favorite spots for quick access
  • Free, no ads, no tracking

Feedback

Karuanas is built by a calisthenics enthusiast for the community. Got feedback, suggestions, or found a bug? Reach out at support@karuanas.eu. As a personal project, responses may take a few days.
